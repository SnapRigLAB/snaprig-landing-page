# ⚡ EMERGENCY FIX: Remove Password & Enable Checkout

## STEP 1: Remove Password Protection (1 minute)

1. Go to: https://admin.shopify.com/store/snapr-3/settings/general
2. Scroll to "Password protection"
3. UNCHECK the box "Enable password"
4. Click "Save" at the bottom
5. ✅ Your store is now accessible to everyone

---

## STEP 2: Check Shopify Payments Status (2 minutes)

1. Go to: https://admin.shopify.com/store/snapr-3/settings/payments
2. Look at status under "Shopify Payments"
3. If it says "Complete account setup" or "Under review":
   - Your checkout is BLOCKED until this is activated
   - You MUST complete Shopify Payments setup
   - This includes: ID verification, bank account, business info

### What each status means:

**"Complete account setup"**
- Checkout: ❌ BLOCKED
- Fix: Complete the setup now
- Time: 15 minutes + 1-3 days approval

**"Under review"** 
- Checkout: ❌ BLOCKED  
- Fix: Wait for Shopify approval
- Time: 1-3 business days
- Check your email for updates

**"Active"**
- Checkout: ✅ WORKING
- No action needed

---

## STEP 3: Create Required Policies (5 minutes)

1. Go to: https://admin.shopify.com/store/snapr-3/settings/legal
2. For EACH policy (Refund, Privacy, Terms, Shipping):
   - Click "Create from template"
   - Review the generated text
   - Click "Save"
3. All 4 must be created or checkout is blocked!

---

## STEP 4: Test Your Checkout

After completing steps 1-3:

1. Open: https://snapr-3.myshopify.com/checkout
2. Should show checkout page (not redirect to homepage)
3. If still redirecting → Shopify Payments not active yet

---

## STEP 5: Alternative - Use PayPal While Waiting

If Shopify Payments is under review, add PayPal now:

1. Go to: https://admin.shopify.com/store/snapr-3/settings/payments
2. Scroll to "Additional payment methods"
3. Click "Add payment method"
4. Select "PayPal"
5. Login to your PayPal account
6. Authorize connection
7. Save

✅ Customers can pay with PayPal immediately while Shopify Payments is being approved
