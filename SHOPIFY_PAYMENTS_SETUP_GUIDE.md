## üÜò COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS

### **Issue 1: "Shopify Payments not available in your region"**

**Solution:**
Romania IS supported by Shopify Payments. But if you see this:
1. Make sure your store address is set to Romania
2. Go to: Settings ‚Üí Store details ‚Üí Store address
3. Update to Romanian address
4. Try activating Shopify Payments again

**Alternative:** Use PayPal
- Settings ‚Üí Payments
- Click "Add payment method"
- Select PayPal
- Connect your PayPal business account

---

### **Issue 2: "Identity verification failed"**

**Solution:**
1. Make sure photos are clear and readable
2. Use a valid Romanian ID (CNP must match your name)
3. Contact Shopify support if it keeps failing
4. Support email: support@shopify.com

---

### **Issue 3: "Bank account not verified"**

**Solution:**
1. Make sure IBAN is correct (starts with RO)
2. Account must be in YOUR name
3. It can take 1-2 business days for verification
4. Check your email for verification steps

---

### **Issue 4: "Store policies missing"**

**Solution:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Legal
2. Create ALL 4 required policies
3. They must be published (not just saved as drafts)
